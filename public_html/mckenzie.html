Our Story.

Orchid Health was founded with the mission of solving the problems and inefficiencies of modern American healthcare, and to make sure that no patient or community lacked access due to the current system. Our founders, Orion Falvey and Oliver Alexander spent years working with a number of Oregon’s top healthcare policy leaders, primary care providers, and most importantly community members themselves in order to improve the health and well-being of all patients and to create a model of health care based on quality, compassion, and innovation.

Exceptional Patient Experience

Our goal is for our patients to feel listened to, have enough time to ask necessary questions, and be able to discuss what’s most important to them with their provider. In order to accomplish this we offer hour long initial visits and 30 minute follow up visits. Additionally we understand the need for accessible primary and urgent care when a health concern arises, which is why we offer same-day visits (by appointment) and expedited new patient visits. In order to continuously improve the experience our patients receive, we constantly ask for feedback and involve patients in our decision making process.

Pricing

At Orchid, we believe you deserve quality healthcare, no matter what. Anyone can be an Orchid patient, whether or not you have insurance. We have many flexible options available to make our care accessible.

We offer a sliding scale program. Review our pricing + apply for the program. If you don’t qualify for our sliding scale program, please review our set cash pay pricing.

Please call us with any questions! We’re happy to walk you through our process.

Note: Note: Orchid Health cannot accept cash payments from Medicaid or Medicare patients in lieu of insurance billing. The cash price services listed are covered by Medicaid and Medicare if your are a Medicaid or Medicare beneficiary.

Your Orchid Care Team

Jodi Budd

Expanded Practice Dental Hygienist

Specialty: Pediatric, Geriatric

Clinical Interests: Oral health care counseling, sealants and fluoride varnish, periodontal therapy and maintenance

Languages: English

Biography:

Jodi graduated from Lane Community College dental hygiene program in Eugene, Oregon in 1990 and received an Associate of Science degree.

She has 29 years clinical experience in general practice and 18 years clinical experience in pediatric specialty practice, Dentistry for Children NW.

Jodi also obtained her Expanded Practice Permit in 2008, which allows a dental hygienist to provide dental hygiene care to patients without the supervision of a dentist.

While continuing to work at Dentistry for Children NW, she started a part-time mobile dental hygiene business, Personal Dental Hygiene Care LLC, providing dental hygiene services for many patients who were homebound and in assisted living and residential care communities, as well as for patients in Oakridge at Five Rivers Family Practice from 2013-2015 and at Orchid Health 2015-2017.

Jodi balances her working life spending time with her husband Dan, her two adult daughters and son-in-law, entertaining family and friends for dinner, parties or BBQ’s, renting houses at the beach or Sunriver, playing guitar, and walking her dog Luna.

Jennifer Checkis

Behavioral Health Clinician & Program Supervisor

Biography:

Jennifer Checkis, LCSW has spent her career providing individual, group, and family therapy to children, adolescents, and adults utilizing cognitive behavioral therapy interventions, systems theory, solution-focused problem-solving techniques, and parent effectiveness training.

She uses play therapy, skill-building and emotional processes while coordinating care with school systems and medical and community professionals to ensure patients receive comprehensive treatment services. Jennifer provides family therapy to facilitate collaborative problem solving, parent effectiveness training and education regarding child development.

Jennifer enjoys relating to a children’s experiences and viewing the entire family system as an integral part of their growth and development. Jennifer is honored to serve the families in the community and looks forward to working with new patients.

When not at work Jennifer can be found gardening, touring around or hiking to beautiful waterfalls, or doing spontaneous things with her 2 young adult sons. Jennifer is also an animal fan and enjoys spending time with her 3 dogs and 2 cats.

Jamie Lloyd

Behavioral Health Consultant, Licensed Professional Counselor

Specialty: Integrated Behavioral Health Care

Clinical Interests: Mental health care in rural settings, psychopharmacology, forensic psychology, trauma-informed care, dual diagnosis, personality disorders.

Languages: English

Education and Certification:

Licensed by Oregon Board of Licensed Professional Counselors and Therapists

Biography:

Jamie grew up in Oakridge and after 24 years away, recently returned to be near family. A graduate of Northwest Christian University, Jamie holds a Master’s Degree in Community Counseling and has worked in various settings including non-profits, integrated medical clinics, corrections, and private practice. She is passionate about collaborative care and enjoys working in a team environment that addresses the needs of the whole individual. Jamie’s experience includes childhood, adolescent, adult, and geriatric care; she has worked with individuals, couples, families, and groups.

Jamie’s family systems approach to treatment focuses on patient-centered care within the context of each individual’s unique perspective of their environment. At Orchid Health, Jamie’s primary focus is providing support to individuals with the goal of better treatment outcomes that enhance quality of life.

Location

Located at the foot of the Cascade Mountains, Oakridge is in the center of one of the best recreational areas in the state and is just 45 minutes east of Eugene, the state's third largest city and home of the University of Oregon. The city is known for year round sunny weather, having over 500 miles of hiking and biking trails, and being surrounded by waterfalls, hot springs and numerous other outdoor adventure opportunities. The Oakridge High School was recently ranked a top high school by US News and World Report. For more information visit the Chamber of Commerce's website
