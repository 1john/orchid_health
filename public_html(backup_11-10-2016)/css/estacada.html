OUR STORY

Our Story.

Orchid Health was founded with the mission of solving the problems and inefficencies of modern American healthcare, and to make sure that no patient or community lacked access due to the current system. Our founders, Orion Falvey and Oliver Alexander spent years working with a number of Oregon’s top healthcare policy leaders, primary care providers, and most importantly the community members in order to improve the health and well-being of all patients and to create a model of health care based on quality, compassion, and innovation.

OUR MODEL

Exceptional Patient Experience.

Our goal is for our patients to feel listened to, have enough time to ask necessary questions, and be able to discuss what’s most important to them with their provider. In order to accomplish this we offer hour long initial visits and 30 minute follow up visits. Additionally we understand the need for accessible primary and urgent care when a health concern arises, which is why we offer same day urgent care visits and expedited new patient visits. In order to continuously improve the experience our patients receive, we constantly ask for feedback and involve patients in our decision making process.

OUR PROVIDERS

Care Team

Lisa Striegle

Family Nurse Practitioner

Specialty: Family Medicine, Adult/Geriatric Medicine, AANP Board-certified

Clinical Interests: Chronic Disease Management, Women's Health and prenatal care, Whole person health, Prevention and health education

Languages: English

Education and Certification:

M.S. Nursing, University of Alabama

Biography:

Lisa was born and raised in southwest Michigan, and after obtaining her nursing degree, moved to a rural health setting to work at a critical access hospital. Here she served as the manager of the obstetrics department, was an infection preventionist, an employee health coordinator, and a certified breast health navigator. In 2012 she earned her Master of Science in Nursing in Infection Prevention and Control and followed this with a second M.S.N. as an Adult/gerontology Primary Care Nurse Practitioner. She has been practicing as a nurse practitioner and a certified care coordinator for the last 1 1/2 years at a rural health clinic in southwest Michigan. She has recently completed further training in pregnancy management and pediatrics to become a certified Family Nurse Practitioner.

Lisa has earned awards in excellence in obstetrics care, academic excellence, and executive nursing excellence. She has proudly accepted a position with Orchid Health to provide a wide scope of primary and preventative care services in the Estacada area.

When not involved in nursing, Lisa enjoys spending time with her husband of 25 years, Brian and their two children, teenagers Bree and Chase. The family looks forward to exploring the Oregon outdoors via biking, hiking, and kayaking.

Lynn Murphy

MD

Specialty: Family Medicine, AAFP Board-certified

Clinical Interests: Chronic Disease Management, Allergy care, Mental health, Nutrition, Whole person health

Languages: English, Spanish

Education and Certification:

M.D. Temple University School of Medicine
UCSF Family Medicine Residency and Internship

Biography:

Dr. Lynn Murphy was raised in Portland, Oregon, and now she is returning home to be near family and friends. During her years away, she worked for many years as a volunteer with inner city children in Philadelphia. She then achieved her Medical Degree through Temple University before returning to the West Coast. After a California Residency at UCSF, she practiced as the only doctor in a small town near the San Francisco Area, successfully operating her own private office for the last 14 years.

For personal and family health reasons, Dr Murphy’s family took up residence in Hawaii in 2015. She now shares time between the two states: Oregon and Hawaii. As a physician, Dr. Murphy relishes practicing “Whole Person Medicine”, thrives in an environment where she can spend quality time with her patients, and is also an excellent diagnostician who is willing to embrace high quality medical standards while still inviting natural health care into the picture. She loves a small town environment and is excited to have joined the team at Orchid Health in Estacada.

When not working you might often find her kayaking on the ocean or possibly Stand Up Paddle- boarding. She also spends a lot of time as a homeschooling parent, a chef for her allergy-ridden son, writing or playing music, and running a successful homespun jewelry business.

Oliver Alexander
