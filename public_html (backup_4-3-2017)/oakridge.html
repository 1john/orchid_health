Our Story.

Orchid Health was founded with the mission of solving the problems and inefficencies of modern American healthcare, and to make sure that no patient or community lacked access due to the current system. Our founders, Orion Falvey and Oliver Alexander spent years working with a number of Oregon’s top healthcare policy leaders, primary care providers, and most importantly the community members in order to improve the health and well-being of all patients and to create a model of health care based on quality, compassion, and innovation.

Our Model.

Exceptional Patient Experience

Our goal is for our patients to feel listened to, have enough time to ask necessary questions, and be able to discuss what’s most important to them with their provider. In order to accomplish this we offer hour long initial visits and 30 minute follow up visits. Additionally we understand the need for accessible primary and urgent care when a health concern arises, which is why we offer same day urgent care visits and expedited new patient visits. In order to continuously improve the experience our patients receive, we constantly ask for feedback and involve patients in our decision making process.

Provider Team

Julie Willardson

FNP

Specialty: Family Medicine, AANP Board-certified

Clinical Interests: Diabetes care, women's health, pediatrics

Languages: English

Education and Certification:

Doctorate of Nursing Practice, University of Utah, 2011

Biography:

Julie moved to Oregon from Salt Lake City, Utah where she worked as an assistant professor of nursing at Roseman University. She started her career in healthcare after graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the University of Utah in May of 2000. Over her career, she has served in many clinical and hospital positions including oncology, diabetes and wound care, and truly found her passion in Home Health and Hospice. It was this love of community nursing and a desire to return to a small town that led her to continue her education, graduating with a Doctorate of Nursing Practice, becoming a Family Nurse Practitioner in May 2011.

Julie focuses on patient education, preventive medicine, and chronic disease management. Practicing medicine at Orchid Health allows her more time to discuss and individualize treatment plans with patients. As a Family Nurse Practitioner, Julie treats children, adolescents, adults and older adults. When she isn’t practicing, she enjoys hiking, camping, and spending time with her husband.

Mary Gabriele

MD

Specialty: Family Medicine, AAFP Board-certified

Clinical Interests: Rural health care, Functional medicine, Nutrition, Geriatrics

Languages: English

Education and Certification:

M.D. Drexel College of Medicine
Bryn Mawr Family Practice Residency
Tacoma Family Medicine Rural Medicine Fellowship

Biography:

Dr. Mary Gabriele, MD originally hails from the East Coast where she was born in Connecticut. She spent quite a few years living in Philadelphia and attended medical school at Drexel College of Medicine from 2000-2004. Following that, she completed her residency at Bryn Mawr Family Practice in Pennsylvania and then completed a fellowship in Rural Medicine at Tacoma Family Medicine in Tacoma, Washington. Today, she serves her patients with over 12 years of experience.

Having visited Oregon in the early 1990s, the state left a great impression on her, leading her to choose to return to Oregon permanently in 2008. Previously, she worked for PeaceHealth as a family medicine physician in Eugene and Cottage Grove until 2015 when she joined Orchid.

Mary’s medical interests include the diagnosis and treatment of chronic diseases in primary care while also advocating for a healthy lifestyle including smoking cessation, diet, and exercise. I’m happy to see patients one year of age and older! Her personal interests include gardening, camping, theater, and reading. Most of all, I enjoy spending time with my new daugther.

Joel Myers

PA-C

Specialty: Family Medicine, Emergency Medicine, NCCPA Certified

Clinical Interests: Urgent care services, sports nutrition

Languages: English

M.S. Medical Science, Emory University

Biography:

Joel is originally from Albuquerque, New Mexico and has lived in Bend, Oregon for 11 years while working in the Emergency Department for St. Charles Medical Centers in Redmond, Madras, and Prineville. Clinic Manager

Location

Located at the foot of the Cascade Mountains, Oakridge is in the center of one of the best recreational areas in the state and is just 45 minutes east of Eugene, the state's third largest city and home of the University of Oregon. The city is known for year round sunny weather, having over 500 miles of hiking and biking trails, and being surrounded by waterfalls, hot springs and numerous other outdoor adventure opportunities. The Oakridge High School was recently ranked a top high school by US News and World Report. For more information visit the Chamber of Commerce's website
